Career Role

As a civil engineer working on a construction simulation game, I would collaborate with developers to ensure realistic structural design and accurate physical behaviors of materials. My expertise would help make the game both educational and engaging for players interested in civil engineering.

Ethical Issue

An ethical issue could arise if the simulation misrepresents engineering safety standards, leading players to believe unsafe construction methods are acceptable. Accuracy and responsibility in the design process are essential to maintain public trust and professional integrity.

Cybersecurity Issues

Two potential cybersecurity challenges include:

  • Data Breaches: User data or internal design files could be compromised through cyberattacks.
  • Intellectual Property Theft: Game designs, models, or proprietary simulation code could be stolen or pirated.

Cybersecurity Best Practices

To protect data and maintain system integrity, developers should use multi-factor authentication, encrypt sensitive data, regularly update software, and conduct security audits. Employee training and secure coding practices also reduce the risk of human error and vulnerabilities.
